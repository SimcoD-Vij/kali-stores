About Us

Your trusted local grocery partner

Welcome to Kali Provisions

For over a decade, Kali Provisions has been serving the Ayanavaram community with quality groceries and household essentials. What started as a small neighborhood store has grown into a trusted wholesale and retail destination for fresh, authentic products.

Our mission is simple: provide high-quality products at affordable prices with the convenience of home delivery. We believe that everyone deserves access to fresh, authentic groceries without compromising on quality or breaking the bank.
